Crystal

Limited-edition 22 x 26 mm Rectangle Watch

in Stainless Steel with White Mother-of-pearl and Diamonds

Price
$3,500
Description & Details

Drawing inspiration from the iconic Tiffany T jewelry collection, Tiffany T limited-edition watches are a distinctive blend of innovative design and expert Swiss craftsmanship. Thoughtfully designed in stainless steel with white mother-of-pearl and diamonds, this rectangular watch looks sleek worn solo or stacked with Tiffany bracelets.

  • Stainless steel, in a limited series of 375
  • White mother-of-pearl dial with applied diamond indexes
  • 22 x 26 mm case with round brilliant diamonds
  • Carat total weight .04
  • Quartz movement
  • Water resistant to 30 meters/100 feet/3 ATM
  • Swiss-made
